ABOUT

Vision and Mission

VIVA College strives to create excellent platform of growth for students with its infrastructure facilities to give birth to an era of Active Alert and Competitive Generation

Our mission is to provide quality education to students. To teach them the value of education and make them believe in the education system.

Objectives of Trust

 To spread education in all its branches.
  To make such education available to all without any distinction of caste, creed, class or community.
 To promote and impart all kind of education including medical education and to establish, assist and conduct educational institutions in rural areas or at such place or places as the Trust may decide from time.
 To provide, maintain, run and construct Hostels, Boarding Houses, and Residential quarters for students and staff.
